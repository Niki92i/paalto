# Code of Conduct

paalto is for people building product systems with human judgment still in the loop. Keep the project rigorous, kind, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct about bugs, risks, and missing evidence.
- Critique artifacts and arguments, not people.
- Assume contributors are operating in good faith.
- Prefer specific examples over broad claims.
- Protect private customer and company data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, customer data, or confidential transcripts.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to bypass the human gates or draft-PR-only rule.
- Spam, low-effort self-promotion, or irrelevant automation pitches.

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, and pull requests to keep the project focused and safe.
